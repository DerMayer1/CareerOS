# Red Flags

Blocking:

- US-only when the candidate lacks US work authorization.
- Onsite-only outside the accepted region.
- Salary below configured minimum.

Warning:

- EU-only with unclear eligibility.
- Long unpaid tests before an initial conversation.
- Very generic role with unclear company or product.
- Hiring language such as "rockstar" or "ninja".

Info:

- Salary not disclosed.
- Company size or stage unknown.
